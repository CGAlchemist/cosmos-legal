# Cosmos Support

We're glad you're here. If something isn't working, or you have a question,
an idea, or feedback about Cosmos, we'd love to hear from you.

## Contact us

Email **cosmosappsupport@gmail.com** and we'll get back to you. Including your
device model and iOS or macOS version helps us help you faster.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is my birth data private?

Yes. Your birth details and the charts calculated from them stay on your
device — we never collect, sync, or transmit them. See the
[Privacy Policy](privacy.html) for the full details.

### How do I manage or cancel my Cosmos+ subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple, not by Cosmos:

- iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions
- macOS: System Settings → [your name] → Media & Purchases → Manage

You can also visit <https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions>.

### I got a new device — how do I get Cosmos+ back?

Open Cosmos, go to Settings, and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you're
signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

### Why does my chart look different from another app?

Different apps use different house systems and rounding conventions. Cosmos
lets you choose your house system in Settings — try matching it to the other
source and the placements should line up.

### How is the daily reading written?

On supported devices, Cosmos composes your reading using Apple's on-device
foundation models. The text is generated locally; your birth data is not sent
to any server to produce it.
