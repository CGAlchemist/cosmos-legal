# Cosmos Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 19, 2026_

Cosmos is built around a simple principle: your astrological life is yours,
and it stays on your device. This policy explains exactly what that means.

## Data we collect

**None.**

We do not have a server, an account system, or analytics. We do not log,
sync, share, sell, or transmit any of the following:

- Your name
- Your birth date, time, or place
- Your birth chart, transits, or readings
- Your calendar entries, notes, or saved horoscopes
- Your subscription status
- Your device identifiers, IP address, or location

## Where your data lives

Everything you enter in Cosmos — birth profiles, calendar events, saved
readings, preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's
SwiftData framework. It is included in your encrypted device backup if
you have iCloud Backup or Time Machine enabled, in which case Apple's
privacy policy governs how that backup is stored.

If you delete the Cosmos app, all data is removed from your device.

## On-device AI

Cosmos+ uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models to compose your
expansive daily readings. Your birth data and the prompt are processed
entirely on your device by Apple's local model. Nothing is sent to
Anthropic, OpenAI, Apple's servers, or any other third party for the
purpose of writing your horoscope.

## Embedded third-party content

The Live Skies section embeds livestreams from public third-party
services (YouTube, AllSkyCam). When you open a livestream, your device
loads video directly from that service. We use YouTube's privacy-
enhanced domain (`youtube-nocookie.com`) where supported.

Those services may set their own cookies and collect their own data
under their respective privacy policies:

- YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- AllSkyCam: https://www.allskycam.com

We have no access to anything those services collect.

## Subscriptions

Cosmos+ is sold as an auto-renewing subscription through the App Store.
Apple handles the entire transaction — payment, billing, renewals,
cancellations, and receipt validation. We never see your payment
details. You can view or cancel your subscription at any time in:

- iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions
- macOS: System Settings → [your name] → Media & Purchases → Manage

## Notifications

If you enable transit notifications (Cosmos+), Cosmos schedules local
notifications on your device using Apple's notification system. The
notification content is composed locally from your birth chart. No
push tokens or notification content are sent to any server.

## Children

Cosmos is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly
collect information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated date at the top will change
and the new version will be posted at this URL.
